Our Story

Youth and Dreams Initiative (YDI)

Based in Adamawa State and founded in 2015, YDI is a community-driven organization focused on empowering young people through mentorship, skills acquisition, education, innovation, and strategic partnerships. Over the past decade YDI has grown from a grassroots vision into a structured development platform.

Our Mission

To empower youth through mentorship, skills acquisition, education, innovation, and strategic partnerships.

  • Mentorship & Leadership
  • Education & Career Outreach
  • Skills Acquisition & Entrepreneurship

Beneficiaries (2015–2025)

10-Year Impact (2015–2025)

Over the last decade, YDI has directly reached over 8,000 beneficiaries through specialized programs:

  • Mentorship & Leadership: 240+ weekly sessions, 1,200+ youth trained
  • Education & Career Outreach: 5,000+ students across 20+ schools
  • Skills Acquisition: 350+ youth trained; 40% launched income-generating ventures
  • Girls Hub (2023): 600+ girls supported
  • Sports Development: 500+ young athletes engaged
  • Psychosocial Support: 120+ at-risk children supported

YDI's core values: Integrity, Excellence, Innovation, Inclusion, Service, Sustainability.

Youth & Dreams Initiative (YDI)

Community-driven youth development platform

Founded in 2015 in Adamawa State, YDI has evolved into a structured platform focused on education, youth empowerment, sports, and community impact. We partner with schools, community leaders, and NGOs to scale our programs.

YDI continues to address challenges such as resource constraints, funding, and sustainability while pursuing community-owned solutions.

Moses Homtapwa

Visioneer & Founder, Youth and Dreams Initiative (YDI)

Moses Homtapwa is a creative and innovative thinker, coach, mentor, and teacher dedicated to the positive transformation of youth and children. As the Visioneer behind YDI, he has spent the last decade building a platform that bridges generations and empowers the next wave of global leaders.

The Visionary Journey

In 2015, Moses founded YDI with a grassroots vision to address the gaps in youth development within Adamawa State. Under his leadership, the organization has grown from an informal mentorship group into a structured development platform that has reached over 8,000 beneficiaries.

Core Philosophy & Impact
  • Mentorship: 240+ weekly sessions
  • Education: 5,000+ students across 20+ schools
  • Sports & Discipline: engaging young athletes through soccer and basketball
  • Innovation: Digital Dreams Program and Skills & Innovation Hub
Education & Expertise

Moses is an alumnus of MAUTech Yola (FUTY) and FCE Yola. He is a digital creator and community advocate with deep roots in Numan, Nigeria.

Strategic Outlook (2026–2035)
  • Dream Impact Fund (DIF) for youth-led startups
  • Expansion of YDI Academy and Community Resource Centers
