Moses Homtapwa

Visioneer & Founder, Youth and Dreams Initiative (YDI)

Moses Homtapwa is a creative thinker, coach, and mentor dedicated to the positive transformation of youth and children. As the Visioneer behind YDI, he has spent the last decade building a platform that bridges generations and empowers the next wave of leaders.

The Visionary Journey

In 2015, Moses founded YDI to address gaps in youth development within Adamawa State. Under his leadership the organization has grown from an informal mentorship group into a structured platform that has reached over 8,000 beneficiaries.

Core Philosophy & Impact
  • Mentorship: 240+ weekly sessions
  • Education: 5,000+ students reached
  • Sports & Discipline: engaging young athletes through soccer and basketball
  • Innovation: Digital Dreams Program and Skills & Innovation Hub
Education & Expertise

Moses is an alumnus of MAUTech Yola (FUTY) and FCE Yola. He is a digital creator and community advocate with deep roots in Numan, Nigeria.

Strategic Outlook (2026–2035)
  • Dream Impact Fund (DIF) for youth-led startups
  • Expansion of YDI Academy and Community Resource Centers
"To raise a generation of purpose-driven, skilled, and socially responsible youth who positively transform their communities."
